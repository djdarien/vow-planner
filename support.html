Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my data?

If you are having issues, you may need to go under your profile and select "Reset All Data." Before doing this, ensure you back up your data by exporting it, even if it is already backed up via iCloud sync.

How do I export my data?

Go to your profile settings and select "Export Data." Follow the prompts to save a copy of your data locally.

What if the app isn't working properly?

If you're experiencing issues, try restarting your device or redownloading the app from the App Store. This can often resolve unexpected glitches. Make sure your app is updated to the latest version.

Contact Support

If you can't find the answer you're looking for, feel free to reach out to us directly. Our team is here to help you with any questions or concerns.
