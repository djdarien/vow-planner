Powerful Features for Your Wedding

Everything you need to plan your perfect day

Timeline Planner

Never miss a moment with our comprehensive timeline planner. Create a detailed schedule that encompasses every aspect of your wedding day - from hair and makeup preparations to the first dance and send-off.

Our intuitive interface allows you to:

  • Schedule multiple events: Add as many timeline items as you need
  • Assign responsibilities: Assign tasks to your bridal party and vendors
  • Set reminders: Get timely notifications before each event
  • Share with your party: Share your timeline with key stakeholders

Guest List Management

Keep your guests organized with our advanced guest list management system. Track RSVPs, manage seating arrangements, and maintain detailed guest profiles.

Features include:

  • RSVP tracking: Monitor who has responded and who needs a reminder
  • Seating charts: Visual drag-and-drop seating arrangements
  • Dietary restrictions: Note special dietary requirements
  • Address management: Store and organize guest contact information

Budget Tracker

Take control of your wedding finances with our comprehensive budget tracker. Set your overall budget, allocate funds to different categories, and monitor spending in real-time.

Track expenses including:

  • Venue and catering: Primary location and food costs
  • Photography and videography: Capture your memories
  • Fashion and beauty: Dress, suits, hair, and makeup
  • Flowers and decor: Create your perfect atmosphere
  • Entertainment: Music and entertainment costs

Planning Checklist

Stay organized throughout your wedding planning journey with our customizable checklist. Start planning 12-18 months in advance and work through every detail.

Our checklist covers:

  • 9-12 months out: Venue booking, vendors selection
  • 6-8 months out: Dress shopping, catering menu
  • 3-5 months out: Invitations, RSVP tracking
  • 1-2 months out: Final details, timelines
  • 1 week out: Final checks, vendor confirmations

Calendar Integration

Seamlessly sync your wedding planning with your iPhone calendar. All your important dates and deadlines appear alongside your personal and professional appointments.

Integration features:

  • Sync all events: automatically add to your calendar
  • reminders: get notifications before key dates
  • Shareable events: invite family and vendors
  • multiple calendar support: work with different calendars

Wedding Website

Create a beautiful, professional wedding website to share details with your guests. Your website serves as the central hub for all wedding information.

Website features:

  • Event information: details about ceremony and reception
  • accommodation: hotel blocks and travel info
  • registry: link to your gift registries
  • FAQ section: answers to common guest questions
  • RSVP functionality: direct response tracking
